## 3. Running a Session

### Recommended Session Format (60-90 minutes)

| Time | Activity | Materials Needed |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| 0-10 min | Welcome & opening prayer | None |
| 10-20 min | Scripture reading | Lesson PDF — Scripture section |
| 20-40 min | Group discussion | IE Answer Keys & Discussion Guide |
| 40-55 min | Activity / reflection writing | Lesson PDF — Reflection section |
| 55-70 min | Share & testimony | Facilitation Notes |
| 70-80 min | Application challenge | Teaching Tips |
| 80-90 min | Closing prayer & announcements | None |

### Tips for Facilitators
- **Read the facilitation notes BEFORE the session** — they highlight common questions and tricky topics
- **Don't read the answer key out loud** — use it to guide discussion, not lecture
- **Encourage journaling** — the reflection questions work best when written first, then discussed
- **Adapt to your group** — Youth groups may need shorter sessions (45-60 min)
- **Mix editions** — IE works alongside AE and YE. Students don't need IE.
